ABOUT US


Today News's independent editorial stand and its reliable and balanced presentation of the news have over the years, won for it the serious attention and regard of the people who matter in India and abroad.

Today News uses modern facilities for news gathering, page composition and printing. It is printed in seventeen centres including the Main Edition at Guwahati (Assam) where the Corporate Office is based. The printing centres at Bangalore, Mumbai, Guwahati, Delhi, Visakhapatnam, Thiruvanathapuram, Kochi, Vijayawada, Mangalore, Tiruchirapalli, Kolkata, Hubli, Mohali,and Allahabad are connected with high speed data lines for news transmission across the country.
